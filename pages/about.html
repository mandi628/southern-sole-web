About Us

We have worked as a team for over 10 years, and have been friends almost as long. Our commitment in our office jobs bonded us in ways we never imagined. After years of working together in our office jobs, we decided it was time to venture out with our own passion.

Tammy took the first leap, and inspired the rest of us to follow our hearts and our spirits to share our excitement for natural therapies with the rest of the world. Now, we offer a complete wellness center, focusing on natural methods of healing ourselves. While we fully respect the medical industry, we realize they can't do everything. We work tirelessly to help our clients be at their best, so their medical treatments can work their best.

Not all of our clients are under strict medical care. Our therapies are often preventative. Most often, clients come for the relaxation and restoration our services provide. And many times, clients return because it's just fun. Check out our Services Page for a complete list of what we offer. We even have group packages!
