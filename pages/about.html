About Us

Our team has been more than just colleagues for over a decade; we have been friends and partners in a shared mission. Having bonded through years of dedication in the corporate world, we discovered that our true strength lay in our mutual passion for wellness and natural restoration.

When Tammy took the first leap to pursue this calling, she inspired the rest of us to follow our hearts. Today, we have transformed that inspiration into a complete wellness center dedicated to the art of natural healing.

At Southern Sole, we believe in a holistic approach to health. While we hold deep respect for the medical industry, we recognize that true wellness often requires a bridge between clinical care and natural therapies. We work tirelessly to help our clients reach their physical and emotional best, creating a foundation of peace that allows all other treatments to work more effectively.

Whether you are seeking preventative care, a professional supplement to medical treatment, or simply a space to experience deep relaxation and restoration, we are here for you. Our sessions are designed to be as enjoyable as they are effective—because we believe healing should be a joy.

Explore our Services Page to see our full list of offerings, including our group packages. We look forward to welcoming you into our circle.

Mandi: Heritage, Healing, and the Art of Zhiva

Guided by a lifelong passion for kitchen medicine and the ancestral wisdom of her Ukrainian heritage, Mandi brings a unique, "folk-forward" approach to modern wellness. While her journey began with the alchemy of essential oils and botanical salves, it evolved into a deep practice of Energy Healing. Through her studies in Reiki, Mandi rediscovered Zhiva—the Universal Life Force—a power she realized had been woven into her daily life and heritage for generations.

Inspired by her family’s resilience and their journey from Ukraine in the 1940s, Mandi’s sessions are more than just treatments; they are a bridge between ancient tradition and modern restoration. Whether you are seeking a custom botanical blend or a restorative energy session, Mandi invites you to rediscover your own sense of "well" through the traditions that bring folk medicine to life.

Explore the Zhiva Herbals collection at our front counter, or schedule a free consultation to discover a path tailored to you.

Callie: From Rescue to Restorer

Born into a "puppy-mill" and rescued by Mandi’s son nearly three years ago, Callie has traveled a long road to find her true calling. Though she was once overwhelmed by the world, she found her sanctuary in our family and discovered a remarkable gift in the process.

Callie is a natural Empath. She has an intuitive talent for reading the room and gently offering her presence to those who need a moment of extra support. While she is still refining her "office manners" at the center, she takes her role as our Gentle Guardian very seriously. She loves "going to work" each day, proving that there is no past so heavy that it cannot be transformed into a future of service and love.
