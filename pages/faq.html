Frequently Asked Questions

We provide a basic website template including a homepage, about page, contact form, and FAQ. Layouts are customizable and can include up to three photos and appointment booking where applicable.

Replace the `src` attributes on the placeholder `` tags in the pages (or add your own images to the `assets` folder). Use relative paths like `assets/your-image.jpg` from the root pages or `../assets/your-image.jpg` from `pages/`.

The current contact page opens the user's mail client using `mailto:`. For server-side delivery without a user mail client, I can integrate a service like Formspree, Netlify Forms, or build a small server endpoint.
